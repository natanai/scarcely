How to survive the wastes

PixelLab-forged icons mark the things you can pick up or talk to.

  1. Move with WASD or the arrow keys. Your speed drops as you carry more.
  2. Interact with E or Enter to grab nearby items or listen to wanderers.
  3. Watch your needs. Forage for food, sip water pools, and warm up with embers before the meters fill.
  4. Keep keepsakes to ease all needs a little when supplies run low.
  5. Save or reload from the buttons above any time; saves live in your browser.
